Ingredients:
  - 1 egg
  - 3/4 cup milk
  - 2 tablespoons melted butter
  - 1 cup flour
  - 1 tablespoon sugar
  - 1 teaspoon baking powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
  1. Beat egg until fluffy.
  2. Add milk and melted butter.
  3. Add dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. Let the mix sit undisturbed for about 15-20 min.
  5. Heat a pan greased with butter.
  6. Pour a small amount of batter into pan.
  7. When ready, turn over and cook the other side.
